Prevention at Home

There are simple, free habits you can practice daily to support brain health:

  • Journaling to strengthen memory and process emotions.
  • Reading or learning new skills to keep the mind active.
  • Regular exercise and balanced nutrition to promote overall health.
  • Mindfulness or meditation to reduce stress and improve focus.

Free Online Tools and Games

Engage with free online activities that challenge memory, logic, and problem-solving:

  • NYT Games (crosswords, spelling bee, puzzles)
  • Lumosity (free version available, cognitive games)
  • BrainHQ (limited free activities)
  • Sudoku.com (logic and pattern recognition)
